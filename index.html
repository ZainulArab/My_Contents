What peace of mind happiness can show Making you and other glow, Nurture it makes it last Forget the troubles of the past Never fear that it will go For it could always grow And then tomorrow there it will be, For happiness can set you free.

    The Past

    The past is the past for a reason That is where it is supposed to say, But some cannot let it go, In their heads, it eats away Until all their focus becomes The person they used to be, The mistake they made in their life. Oh! If they could see it. That you cannot change what happened, No matter how hard you try, No matter how much you think about it, No matter how much you cry. What happens in your lifetime, Happens for reason unknowns, So you have to let the cards unfold Let your story be shown. Don't get wrapped in the negative, Be happy with what you have been given, Give for today, not tomorrow, Get up, Get out, and start living. Because the past is the past for a reason, It's been and now it is gone, So stop trying to think of ways to fix it, It's done: it's unchangeable, so move on!

    Zainul Arab OH
